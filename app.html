    Drunkard game is a card game that lies in drawing cards and then comparing them.

    1. Each player randomly gets the half of the deck either red or black.

    2. Then players draw their top card at the same time. Player who drew the card with the higher value gets both of the cards to their deck. When players draw the cards with the same values then 'battle' begins.

    3. They should draw three more cards and then whose third card has higher value gets all of the cards. If one of the players doesn't have enough cards for the 'battle' other player should borrow them their cards.

    4. Apart from that there is one more rule regarding cards' values: the Aces are higher than any card except from the 6 card. And the 6 cards are smaller than any card from the deck but Aces.

    5. The more cards you have the better. Wins the person who can get the whole deck.
